Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Reso ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. Our core philosophy is simple: What happens on your Mac, stays on your Mac. This Privacy Policy explains how our macOS application handles your data.

2. Local Processing

Reso is designed as a privacy-first application. All voice processing, transcription, and text generation occurs directly on your device using the Apple Neural Engine and local AI models.

  • We do not upload your voice recordings to any cloud server.
  • We do not send your transcribed text to any third party.
  • Your content remains entirely on your local machine.

3. Optional Analytics (Your Choice)

To help us improve Reso, you may choose to share anonymous usage statistics. This is entirely optional and can be toggled on or off in the app settings at any time.

We use TelemetryDeck to collect anonymized usage statistics. This helps us understand feature usage frequency (e.g., session starts). All data is anonymized and does not contain your voice, text, or any personally identifiable information.

If enabled, we may collect:

  • App usage patterns (e.g., feature usage frequency, session duration)
  • Device type and macOS version
  • Crash reports and performance metrics

We never collect:

  • Your voice recordings or transcribed content
  • Personal identifiable information
  • Data from other applications

4. Subscription & Purchase Data

We process subscriptions securely through RevenueCat and the Apple App Store. To verify your Pro subscription status and sync your entitlements across devices, we collect:

  • Purchase History – subscription type and expiration date
  • Anonymous Device Identifier – to sync Pro status across your devices

This data is used solely for functionality and is not linked to your personal identity. We do not have access to your credit card or full financial information—Apple handles all billing.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses basic analytics to measure site traffic. This does not connect to the Reso application itself. The application is completely isolated from website tracking.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data on our servers, you already own 100% of your data on your local machine. You can delete the app and all associated data at any time. If you've enabled optional analytics, disabling it will stop all future data collection.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: dev@dzgapp.com.
