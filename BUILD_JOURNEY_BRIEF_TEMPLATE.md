The more concrete this brief is, the less rewriting is needed later.

## 8) Output Requirements for Agent

- Write `zh` first in sincere diary-sharing tone.
- Generate `en` with natural expression (not rigid literal translation).

- Keep zh-TW synchronized (manual or conversion verification).
